FAQ

Charging, safety, warranty, and customization.

Which chemistry should I choose?

LFP for longevity and safety; NMC for higher energy density. We also support mixed approaches depending on duty cycle and cost targets.

Do you provide custom enclosures?

Yes—wall‑mount, rack, and vehicle‑specific enclosures with custom connectors and harnesses.

What warranties are available?

Typical 12–36 months depending on application and usage profile. Extended options on request.

Can you integrate with my inverter?

We support common hybrid inverters and can advise on communication settings for optimal performance.
